# Component Choices

## 1. Chassis
We plan to 3D print the chassis. We are currently exploring different designs to find the most suitable and efficient one.

## 2. Wheels
We are considering the following wheel configurations:
- Four rubber tread wheels
- Four soft wheels
- A combination: two soft wheels (rear) and two rubber tread wheels (front)

## 3. DC Motor
The choice of DC motor will depend on the final selection of other components. A specific model has not yet been determined.

## 4. Power Supply
The power supply will also be selected based on the requirements of the chosen components. No final decision has been made at this stage.
